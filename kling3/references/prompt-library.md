# Prompt Library --- Ready-to-Use Templates

Copy these templates, fill in the bracketed sections, and paste into Kling 3.0. Each template follows the Five-Layer Formula (Scene > Characters > Action > Camera > Audio).

---

## 1. UGC Product Review (Kitchen/Bathroom)

The gold standard for authentic social media ads. This pattern has the highest conversion rate for product content.

```
A handheld, front-facing smartphone shot in a bright, slightly messy
home kitchen. Natural daylight from a nearby window. Mild handheld
shake, authentic phone camera vibe.

A woman in casual clothes stands by the counter, relaxed and unscripted.

(0-3s) She lifts a [PRODUCT] toward the camera, almost filling the frame.
(3-6s) She tilts the [PRODUCT] closer to the lens, rotating it so the
label is visible.
(6-9s) Quick [USE ACTION --- sip/apply/open]. She reacts honestly,
eyebrows lifting.
(9-12s) She lowers the [PRODUCT], leaning toward the camera as if
sharing a secret.

Camera: Static with natural handheld micro-movements. Eye-level.
Vertical 9:16 framing.

[Woman, natural conversational tone]: "Okay wait... I just tried this."
After a beat, [Woman, curious impressed]: "[REACTION LINE]."
Then, [Woman, casual friendly]: "[ENDORSEMENT LINE]."
Ambient: Quiet kitchen tone, subtle [PRODUCT SOUNDS].
Natural ending, like she's about to stop recording.

-no blur, -no flickering, -no morphing
```

### Key UGC Patterns to Remember
- "Handheld, front-facing smartphone shot" = instant UGC feel
- "Slightly messy" environment = authenticity
- Physical action between EVERY line of dialogue
- Discovery arc: try > surprise > describe > endorse
- "As if sharing a secret" = viewer intimacy
- "Natural ending, like about to stop recording" = real creator vibe

## 2. Premium Product Hero (Studio)

For e-commerce, brand films, and any scene where the product is the star.

```
A sleek studio environment with a seamless white cyclorama. Soft
diffused top lighting with subtle edge lighting from the right.
The product sits centred on a matte black pedestal.

(0-4s) Camera begins low angle, slowly rising in a crane movement.
(4-8s) Smooth 180-degree orbit around the product at eye level.
(8-12s) Push-in to extreme close-up of product label/detail.

No dialogue. Ambient: Soft electronic hum, subtle whoosh on camera
moves. Premium, minimal aesthetic. Shallow depth of field.

-no blur, -no flickering, -no text warping, -no morphing
```

## 3. Before/After Demo (Skincare/Beauty)

```
Bright bathroom with soft ring light. Clean, clinical feel.

Shot 1 (4s): Close-up of bare skin showing texture. Flat, unflattering
lighting. Clinical documentation feel.
[Character, disappointed flat voice]: "This is what I woke up to."

Shot 2 (3s): Hands applying the product, gentle patting motion.
ASMR sounds of product application.

Shot 3 (4s): Same angle as Shot 1, but skin appears smoother.
Warmer lighting. Character smiles into camera.
[Character, amazed happy]: "Twenty minutes later... just look."

Shot 4 (4s): Pull back to medium shot. Character confidently touches
face, turns side to side. Warm colour grading.

-no blur, -no morphing, -no flickering
```

## 4. Atmospheric Slow Motion (Fashion/Luxury)

For cinematic brand content where mood matters more than product close-ups.

```
[LOCATION --- e.g. "A quiet Japanese garden at dawn. Cherry blossom
petals drift through soft morning mist. Cedar wood structures, stone
lanterns, still water."]

[CHARACTER --- e.g. "A woman in a tailored cream linen coat, hair
pulled back, minimal gold jewellery."]

(0-5s) She walks slowly through the space. Coat lifts and floats
gently behind her in the breeze. Hair moves naturally.
(5-10s) She pauses. Turns her head slightly. Petals drift past her.
A moment of stillness.

Camera: Wide establishing shot, slowly dollying in. At (5s), shifts
to medium close-up. Shallow depth of field throughout. 96fps
slow-motion feel.

No dialogue. Ambient: [ENVIRONMENT SOUNDS --- rustling leaves, distant
water, birdsong]. Muted colour palette, slight film grain, cinematic
widescreen framing.

-no blur, -no flickering, -no camera shake
```

## 5. Multi-Shot Product Unboxing

```
Bright, airy apartment. Natural window light, authentic phone
camera feel.

Shot 1 (3s): Hands place a sealed box on a clean table. Close-up.
Soft thud sound.

Shot 2 (4s): Hands open the box, revealing product inside. Eyes
widen with discovery. Satisfying unboxing sounds --- paper rustle,
box click.
[Person, casual excited]: "Okay, the packaging alone..."

Shot 3 (4s): Person lifts product out, holds toward camera, turns
it slowly. Light catches the surface.
[Person, impressed]: "This feels really premium."

Shot 4 (4s): Product placed on surface. Final beauty shot. Camera
orbits slowly. Warm ambient light.

Ambient: Soft room tone, packaging rustles, satisfying tactile sounds.
Handheld feel, vertical 9:16.

-no blur, -no morphing, -no flickering
```

## 6. Emotional Close-Up Portrait

For storytelling, brand films, or any scene focused on human emotion.

```
Soft natural light from a single window. Shallow depth of field.
Simple neutral background --- a plain wall, slightly out of focus.

A [PERSON DESCRIPTION], [EXPRESSION --- "between sadness and
acceptance" / "quiet determination" / "barely contained joy"].

(0-5s) Static close-up. They breathe. Subtle micro-expressions shift.
A blink. Jaw tightens slightly.
(5-8s) Camera slowly dollies in. Their eyes grow glossy.
(8-10s) The faintest smile breaks through. They look down, then back
at camera.

[Person, quiet voice]: "[SINGLE LINE --- keep it short and honest]"
Ambient: Room silence, distant muffled sounds, soft breathing.
Warm colour grading, shallow focus, intimate framing.

-no blur, -no flickering
```

## 7. Food/Beverage Pour Shot

```
A rustic kitchen counter with warm afternoon light streaming through
a window. Cutting board, fresh herbs, and the [PRODUCT] bottle.

(0-3s) Static wide shot establishing the scene. Dust particles in
the light beam.
(3-6s) Hand reaches in, picks up the bottle, tilts it.
(6-9s) Pouring into a glass --- liquid catches the light, condensation
on glass. Close-up.
(9-12s) Pull back. Steam or condensation rises. Final beauty shot.

No dialogue. Ambient: Liquid pouring sounds, gentle kitchen ambience,
birds outside. Warm colour palette, shallow depth of field,
food photography aesthetic.

-no blur, -no morphing, -no colour shift
```

## 9. Cinematic Establishing Shot (No Character)

For opening shots, transitions between scenes, or pure atmosphere.

```
[LOCATION with rich detail --- e.g. "A rain-soaked Tokyo alley at
night. Neon signs reflect in puddles. Steam rises from a street vent.
A single red paper lantern swings gently in the wind."]

(0-5s) Wide shot. Camera slowly dollies forward through the space.
Rain falls steadily.
(5-10s) Camera pushes through into a tighter corridor. Neon colours
intensify. Puddle splashes from an unseen passerby.

No dialogue. No characters. Ambient: Rain hitting pavement, distant
traffic, muffled music from behind a door, occasional drip.
Cinematic widescreen, desaturated with warm accent tones, slight
film grain.

-no blur, -no text overlays, -no watermarks
```

## Image-to-Video Templates

These are specifically for when you already have a still image and want to animate it. Remember: **describe ONLY what moves and changes.**

### Animate a Fashion Still
```
She begins to walk slowly forward. Coat fabric lifts and sways
gently with each step. Hair shifts with natural movement. Heels
click softly on [SURFACE].

Camera: Slow push-in from medium to medium close-up. Shallow DOF.
96fps slow-motion feel.

No dialogue. Ambient: Footsteps, fabric rustle, [ENVIRONMENT SOUND].
Film grain, cinematic colour.

-no blur, -no flickering, -no face change
```

### Animate a Product Still
```
Camera begins a slow 90-degree orbit around the product. Light
catches the surface, creating moving highlights and reflections.
[ENVIRONMENTAL ELEMENT --- steam rises / condensation drips / dust
particles drift through light beam].

No dialogue. Ambient: Subtle [RELEVANT SOUND]. Premium aesthetic.

-no product deformation, -no morphing, -no label blur
```

### Animate a Portrait Still
```
Subtle micro-expressions: a slow blink, the faintest smile forming,
a slight head tilt. Breathing visible in chest rise. Hair moves
almost imperceptibly.

Camera: Very slow dolly-in. Almost imperceptible movement.

No dialogue. Ambient: Soft room tone, distant muffled sounds.
Intimate, shallow focus.

-no face change, -no expression exaggeration, -no morphing
```
