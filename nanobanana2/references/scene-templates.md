# Scene Templates --- Copy and Customise

Each template below is a complete prompt you can copy, fill in the bracketed sections, and paste directly into Kie.ai. Always attach your character reference image when generating.

---

## Template 1: Portrait / Talking Head

Best for social media content, testimonial-style shots, profile images, and any scene focused on a person's face and upper body.

```
Photorealistic 9:16 vertical frame. Ultra-realism master execution.
Shot on iPhone 15 Pro front camera, natural wide-angle distortion,
authentic smartphone capture.

Subject: [AGE] [GENDER], [EXPRESSION --- e.g. "warm confident smile",
"calm composed expression"], [POSE --- e.g. "facing camera with slight
head tilt"], wearing [WARDROBE --- describe fabric type, colour, fit,
e.g. "oversized cream cable-knit sweater, gold small hoop earrings"].

Environment: [LOCATION], [TIME OF DAY], [BACKGROUND ELEMENTS ---
e.g. "modern apartment, white walls, large window in background,
potted plant visible"]. Background 6-10 feet behind subject.

Lighting: [LIGHT SOURCE] from [DIRECTION], [QUALITY --- soft/hard],
[COLOUR TEMP], creating [SHADOW DESCRIPTION on face].

Framing: Medium close-up, subject centred with slight offset to
[left/right], eye-level angle, shallow depth of field blurring
background.

SKIN: Visible pores, fine micro-bumps, faint natural redness on
cheeks. Soft specular with T-zone oiliness. Healthy clean skin.
HAIR: Baby hairs at hairline, natural strand separation, no helmet hair.
EYES: Catchlights matching the [window/ring light/etc], subtle moisture
line, realistic iris texture. No glowing rings.
ASYMMETRY: Very subtle brow and cheek differences. No identity change.
FABRIC: Visible [knit/weave] pattern, natural fold tension, micro-wear.
CAMERA FINISH: Mild shadow noise, slight smartphone processing texture,
gentle micro-contrast. No HDR halos.

Subject is NOT speaking. Mouth naturally closed or slight parting.

Keep everything identical: same identity, expression, pose, angle,
lighting. Enhance only micro-details.

--neg no plastic skin, no extra fingers, no warped text, no CGI look,
no identity change, no uncanny eyes, no floating objects, no watermark,
no beauty filter, no helmet hair, no glowing irises
```

## Template 2: Product Hero Shot

Best for e-commerce imagery, product launches, catalogue shots, and any scene focused on an object/product without a person.

```
Photorealistic 9:16 vertical frame. Ultra-realism master execution.
Shot on Sony A7IV with 90mm macro lens, f/8, professional product
photography.

Product: [PRODUCT NAME/TYPE], [SIZE/DIMENSIONS feel], [MATERIAL ---
glass/plastic/metal/fabric/etc], [COLOUR/FINISH], [KEY VISUAL
FEATURES], [LABEL TEXT if any --- keep to 3-5 words max].

Surface: [WHAT THE PRODUCT SITS ON --- marble countertop, wood table,
white seamless, fabric backdrop, etc].

Background: [Simple and clean --- gradient, solid colour, or contextual
environment behind the product].

Props: [Minimal supporting elements if any --- a leaf, a shadow, a
texture swatch. Keep sparse.]

Lighting: [LIGHTING SETUP --- softbox from left, natural window light,
ring light overhead], creating [HIGHLIGHT PLACEMENT on product],
[SHADOW DIRECTION], [how reflections behave on glossy surfaces].

Framing: [SHOT TYPE --- hero straight-on, 45-degree angle, overhead
flat lay], product [centred / rule of thirds], [NEGATIVE SPACE ---
where the empty space is].

PRODUCT MATERIAL: Micro-texture appropriate to material, subtle surface
variation. Tiny edge wear consistent with real handling. Glossy surfaces
show faint fingerprint smudges and 2-3 micro dust specks in highlights.
Matte surfaces show subtle variation. Realistic specular matching the
material. No CGI mirror look.

GROUNDING: Contact shadow directly beneath product, darkest at base,
fading outward. Shadow softness matches the light source.

CAMERA FINISH: Clean controlled lighting response, accurate colour
reproduction, sharp focus on product with appropriate depth falloff.

--neg no warped text, no misspellings, no unreadable text, no logo
distortion, no CGI look, no floating objects, no plastic texture on
natural materials, no watermark
```

## Template 3: Lifestyle B-Roll

Best for brand storytelling, environmental context shots, person-in-their-world scenes. Feels candid, not posed.

```
Photorealistic 9:16 vertical frame. Ultra-realism master execution.
Shot on Fujifilm X-T5 with 35mm f/1.4, natural film simulation,
lifestyle photography aesthetic.

Scene: [ACTIVITY --- e.g. "woman browsing records in a vintage shop",
"man making pour-over coffee in a sunlit kitchen"]. Mood: [VIBE ---
relaxed, energetic, contemplative, playful].

Subject: [AGE] [GENDER], [EXPRESSION --- natural, not posed, candid
feeling], [WHAT THEY ARE DOING], wearing [WARDROBE with fabric details
--- e.g. "worn-in denim jacket over white cotton tee, silver pendant
necklace"].

Environment: [SPECIFIC LOCATION with details --- architecture, objects,
surfaces, textures that tell a story], [TIME OF DAY], [background
activity if any --- other people, street life, etc].

Lighting: [NATURAL or MIXED lighting], [QUALITY and DIRECTION], [how
it falls across the scene], [COLOUR TEMP and MOOD it creates].

Framing: [SHOT TYPE], subject placed [where in frame], [CAMERA ANGLE
--- often slightly off-centre, candid feeling], [DEPTH --- foreground
element / subject / background layers].

SKIN: Visible pores, natural texture, subtle shine. Clean but not
airbrushed.
HAIR: Natural strand separation, flyaways. Real hair behaviour.
FABRIC: Visible weave/texture, natural creasing, micro-wear appropriate
to the garment's age/style.
GROUNDING: Contact shadows at feet, objects on surfaces. Everything
properly placed in the environment.
CAMERA FINISH: Fine film-like grain, subtle halation on highlights,
natural bokeh, clean colour transitions.

AUTHENTICITY: Natural, unposed feeling. Environmental storytelling.
Realistic interaction with space and objects. Candid expression.

Subject is NOT speaking. Natural relaxed expression.

Keep everything identical: same identity, expression, pose, angle,
lighting. Enhance only micro-details.

--neg no plastic skin, no extra fingers, no CGI look, no identity
change, no uncanny eyes, no floating objects, no watermark, no posed
model look, no stock photo feel
```

## Template 4: Hands + Product Interaction

Best for product demos, application shots, unboxing moments, and any scene where hands are the primary subject. **Hands are the highest-risk area for AI artefacts --- use this template carefully.**

```
Photorealistic 9:16 vertical frame. Ultra-realism master execution.
Shot on Sony A7IV with 50mm f/1.8, natural product interaction
photography.

Interaction: [DESCRIBE WHAT THE HANDS ARE DOING --- e.g. "hands
applying serum to face", "fingers opening a product box", "hand
holding a coffee cup mid-sip"].

Hands: [GENDER PRESENTATION], [AGE-APPROPRIATE --- smooth/textured],
[NAIL STYLE --- natural/manicured/short], [SKIN TONE], [POSITION AND
GRIP --- how the fingers wrap around or interact with the object].

Product: [PRODUCT DETAILS --- type, material, size, shape, colour,
label text if visible (3-5 words max)].

Environment: [CONTEXT --- bathroom counter, kitchen table, desk,
outdoor setting], [SUPPORTING ELEMENTS --- other objects that anchor
the scene].

Lighting: [HOW LIGHT FALLS on hands and product --- avoid harsh hand
shadows obscuring the product], [HIGHLIGHT and SHADOW placement].

Framing: [CLOSE-UP on hands + product / wider context showing arms],
[ANGLE --- from above, eye level, slightly below], [DEPTH --- what's
sharp, what's blurred].

HAND ANATOMY (CRITICAL):
- EXACTLY 5 fingers per hand, each clearly separated
- Realistic knuckle structure and natural bending joints
- Natural nail shape with visible cuticles
- Fine lines across knuckles, subtle skin texture at joints
- Fingers wrap naturally around the object
- Subtle pressure marks where skin contacts product
- Fingers correctly go behind the object where appropriate

PRODUCT: Micro-texture, realistic material response, subtle wear.
GROUNDING: Contact shadows where product touches surface, where
hands touch product.

Keep everything identical. Enhance only micro-details.

--neg no extra fingers, no fused fingers, no missing fingers, no
melted joints, no warped hands, no impossible grip, no floating
hands, no anatomical errors, no plastic skin, no CGI look, no
watermark
```

## Template 5: UI / Screen Mockup

Best for app screenshots, website mockups, notification displays, and any scene showing a screen. **AI struggles with text --- keep it large and minimal.**

```
Photorealistic 9:16 vertical frame. Ultra-realism master execution.
Shot on iPhone 15 Pro, screen capture aesthetic with device context.

Device: [PHONE / TABLET / LAPTOP], [SPECIFIC MODEL if it matters],
[HOW IT IS PRESENTED --- held in hands, lying on desk, propped on
stand].

Screen Content:
- UI Type: [App interface / website / notification / text message]
- Layout: SIMPLIFIED --- large text blocks only, no tiny elements
- Text: "[EXACT TEXT --- maximum 3-5 words per element, LARGE SIZE]"
- Colours: [UI COLOUR SCHEME --- use high contrast for readability]
- NO small print, NO tiny buttons, NO complex interfaces

Context: [ENVIRONMENT around the device --- hands holding it, desk
surface with objects, lifestyle setting].

Lighting: [How light interacts with the screen --- avoid harsh glare],
[ambient environment lighting].

Framing: [DEVICE PROMINENCE in frame], [ANGLE --- straight on is best
for text readability], [how much context is visible around the device].

SCREEN REALISM:
- Pixel-perfect text (large text only)
- Realistic screen brightness and colour
- Subtle screen reflections if appropriate
- Accurate device frame details (buttons, camera cutout)

DEVICE REALISM:
- Accurate proportions and physical details
- Realistic material (glass, metal frame)
- Proper finger interaction if being held (see C8 for hand rules)

GROUNDING: Device properly resting on surface with contact shadow.

TEXT IS CRITICAL: All on-screen text must be LARGE, HIGH CONTRAST,
PERFECTLY READABLE, and CORRECTLY SPELLED. When in doubt, use fewer
words in bigger text.

--neg no warped text, no misspellings, no blurry text, no unreadable
text, no tiny text, no complex UI, no extra fingers, no CGI look,
no watermark
```
